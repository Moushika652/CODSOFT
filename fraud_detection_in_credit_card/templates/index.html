Project Overview

Building intelligent systems to protect financial transactions from fraudulent activities

Problem Statement

Credit card fraud is one of the most common financial crimes. With only 0.172% of transactions being fraudulent, traditional models struggle to detect patterns effectively.

ML Solution

Our system uses advanced machine learning techniques including Random Forest, SMOTE for balancing, and comprehensive evaluation metrics to ensure reliable detection.

Expected Outcome

High precision and recall in fraud detection while minimizing false positives, providing reliable protection for genuine users and financial institutions.

Our Approach

Systematic methodology for building an effective fraud detection system
