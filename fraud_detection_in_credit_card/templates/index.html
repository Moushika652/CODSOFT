Overview

What is Credit Card Fraud?

Credit card fraud occurs when someone uses your credit card information without your permission to make purchases or withdraw money. This can happen through stolen cards, data breaches, or identity theft.

How Our System Works

Our fraud detection system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze transaction patterns and identify suspicious activities. It considers factors like transaction amount, time, location, and transaction type to calculate a risk score.

Key Risk Factors

  • • High transaction amounts (over ₹5,000)
  • • Unusual transaction times (before 6 AM or after 10 PM)
  • • International or online transactions
  • • Multiple transactions in short time periods
